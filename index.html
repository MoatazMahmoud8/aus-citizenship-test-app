Real Exam Format

Know What to Expect

  • 📋
    20 Multiple-Choice Questions Selected from the full question bank, each with 3–4 options.

    5 Mandatory Values Questions You must answer ALL 5 Australian values questions correctly to pass — regardless of your total score.
  • ✅
    75% Pass Mark You need at least 15 out of 20 correct answers, AND all 5 values questions correct.

    Based on "Our Common Bond" All questions come directly from the official Australian Government resource booklet.
