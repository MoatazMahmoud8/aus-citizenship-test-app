Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 March 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Australian Citizenship Test App (the "App") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and providing a clear, reliable study experience while you prepare for the Australian citizenship test.

1 Information We Collect

The App is designed to be a privacy‑friendly educational tool.

Personal information

The App does not require you to create an account and does not ask for your name, address, phone number, or other personal identifiers.

Usage data stored on your device

The App may save your practice test scores, progress, and similar study information locally on your device so you can track your learning over time. This information is stored only on your device and is not sent to us or to our own servers.

2 Permissions

To operate correctly, the App may request the following permissions:

  • Internet access – used to download updated question banks and to open external links to official study materials, such as the Australian Government's "Our Common Bond" resources.
  • Storage / local data – used to save your progress, settings, and high scores on your device.

The App does not access your photos, contacts, location, or other sensitive information.

3 Third‑Party Services

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties.

If you access content that is hosted on GitHub Pages as part of the App's website, please note that GitHub may collect limited technical information (such as IP address, browser type, and basic usage logs) for security, performance, and analytical purposes in accordance with GitHub's own privacy practices.

4 Children's Privacy

The App is intended for people preparing for the Australian Citizenship Test. It is not designed for children under 13 years of age, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

5 Data Security

We use standard technical and organisational measures to help protect the App and any information stored on your device. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
